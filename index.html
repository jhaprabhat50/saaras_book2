Meet the author

Saara Sarkazi

It started in a classroom. Whenever her teacher handed out a writing prompt, Saara's mind would wander off the page — into new characters, unexpected adventures, and worlds that didn't exist yet. Reading was fun, but she wanted more: she wanted to build the worlds herself.

One day she told her dad she wanted to write her own stories. He listened, smiled, and told her to start. He promised that if she kept writing, he'd help get her stories printed one day — and that promise became the spark for this book. She wrote through her holidays in Mumbai and kept going once she was back at school, filling pages with characters entirely her own.

"The thought that I could be an author sounded so exciting — I couldn't wait to begin."

Saara's Book of Stories is the result: ten stories written between the ages of nine and ten, each one carrying a small lesson learned along the way.
